Our Company

We are essentially a small family owned and run business that has been operating in this way since mid-2014. There are a number of benefits of working with a family business in addition to the fact that we are a happy committed team who love doing what we do. Among the benefits felt by our clients are the fact that our flexibility to work different hours to larger organisations means that we can better meet the needs of our clients. Because we don't operate from large expensive premises, we don’t have large overheads, which in turn means that we are very competitively priced.


The fact that we are a small business does not govern the size of the clients that we are able to support. Working across both the Public and Private Sectors, our team have supported clients large and small - from the small family-owned bakery to global FMCG giants and the European Regulator of Human and Veterinary Medicinal Products. We also undertake subcontracting work for other consulting businesses who are looking to bring additional skills to a commission.
